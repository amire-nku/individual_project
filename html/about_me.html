About the Creator


My name is Amir! Some call me Sylk online as he is my persona/mascot for my business!

More information about me and why you should consider comissioning me!


I am a freelance artist who tends to have lower prices for any who wish to get high-quality pieces. Since this is my hobby I tend to lower the prices compared to my competition. If I worked full-time as and artist I probably would raise the amount per hour. :)

If you are looking to get your characters drawn whether for a book or just for fun, my art may be for you! I specialize mainly in character designing! I've done work for many fellow artists as well as streamers that need a new mascot!

If you are looking to go into the career of streaming partial or full-time then you also will be glad to hear I do a lot of work with professional streamers! Ranging from setting up their profiles with pictures, but also giving virtual mascots (v-tubers) a model to show on stream instead of their face!

I can even do thumbnails for videos if needed!


Another reason why you would want to support me is that I am heavily against using AI in the art industry! A lot of clients are always worried that they aren't getting authentic work, but with my services you will never be handed anything less than pure human-made art! Passion and all!


You can find examples of my work for streaming and character designs in the navigation menu! Feel free to check those out and see if you like my style!

The Creator

I started drawing as a way to help express myself as well as assist with others who may not be able to create what they envision. I strive to always make my clients happy so that I may share my talent with more people in the future.

Although I love doing commissions, they're not my full-time activity. I also put my art skills towards creating videogames or designs that support my literature.
